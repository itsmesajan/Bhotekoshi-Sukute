Activities at Sukute Resort

Experience a blend of adventure and relaxation with our diverse range of activities, perfect for families and corporate groups.

Adventure Activities

Rafting

Navigate the thrilling rapids of the Bhotekoshi River with our guided rafting tours.

Kayaking

Explore the serene waters with our kayaking sessions, suitable for all skill levels.

Canyoning

Descend through waterfalls and natural rock formations with our expert-led canyoning adventures.

Recreational Activities

Beach Volleyball

Enjoy a friendly game on our sandy court.

Table Tennis

Challenge your friends to a tournament.

Swimming Pool

Relax and unwind in our refreshing pool.

Campfire

Storytelling and marshmallow roasting under the stars.

Unique Experiences

Cultural Performances

Immerse yourself in the local culture with traditional dance and music performances.

Nature Walks

Discover the natural beauty of the surrounding area with guided nature walks.
